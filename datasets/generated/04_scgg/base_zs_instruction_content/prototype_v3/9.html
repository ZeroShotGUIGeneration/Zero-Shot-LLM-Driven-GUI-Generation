Classic Italian Lasagna

1 hr 30 mins Moderate

This classic Italian lasagna is a perfect dish for family dinners. Layered with rich meat sauce and creamy béchamel, it's a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with chopped basil, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta mixture. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce.
  7. Top with shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Nutrition Information

Serving Size: 1 piece Calories: 450 Fat: 20g Protein: 25g Allergens: Dairy, Wheat
