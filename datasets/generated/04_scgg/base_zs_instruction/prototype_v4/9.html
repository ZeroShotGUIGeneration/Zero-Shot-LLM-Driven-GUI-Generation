Delicious Recipe

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins Moderate
By Chef John Doe

This recipe is a classic dish from Italy. Perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Step 2: Mix flour , sugar , and salt in a bowl.
  3. Step 3: Add eggs and milk , and stir until smooth.
  4. Step 4: Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  5. Step 5: Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Step 6: Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!

Nutrition Information

Serving Size: 1 piece Calories: 250 Fat: 10g Protein: 5g Allergens: Eggs, Milk
