Spaghetti Carbonara
Recipe Steps
  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 1 Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  • 2 Step 2: Fry the pancetta with the garlic.
  • 3 Step 3: Beat the eggs in a bowl, then add the cheeses and mix.
  • 4 Step 4: Drain the spaghetti and add to the pancetta. Remove from heat and mix in the egg mixture.
  • 5 Step 5: Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
