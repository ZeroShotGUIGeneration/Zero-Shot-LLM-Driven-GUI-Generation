Classic Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and parsley.
  6. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, lasagna noodles, and ricotta mixture. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for 15 minutes before serving.
