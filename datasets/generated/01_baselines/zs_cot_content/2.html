Q: What should I wear to a summer wedding?

A: Light and airy fabrics like linen or cotton are great choices. Consider pastel colors or floral prints.

Q: Are skinny jeans still in style?

A: Skinny jeans are a classic and can still be stylish when paired with the right top and accessories.

Q: How can I style a denim jacket?

A: Denim jackets are versatile. You can pair them with dresses, skirts, or even other denim pieces for a chic look.

Q: What accessories go well with a little black dress?

A: Statement jewelry, a stylish clutch, and elegant heels can elevate your little black dress.
