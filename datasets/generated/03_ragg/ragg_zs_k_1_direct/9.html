Ingredients required

375g dried fettuccine
25g butter
4 garlic cloves, crushed
300ml thickened cream
2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley leaves
1/3 cup finely grated parmesan cheese or vegetarian hard cheese

Directions for preparation

Step 1: Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions.
Step 2: Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat, then add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
Step 3: Add the cream and bring to a simmer, then stir in the parmesan cheese.
Step 4: Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce and toss to coat.
Step 5: Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
