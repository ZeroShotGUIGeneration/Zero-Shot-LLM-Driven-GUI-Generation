Classic Fettuccine Alfredo

Prep Time

15 min

Total Time

25 min

Serves

4

Ingredients required

  • + 375g dried fettuccine
  • + 25g butter
  • + 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • + 300ml thickened cream
  • + 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley leaves
  • + 1/3 cup finely grated parmesan cheese or vegetarian hard cheese

Directions for preparation

  • 1. Cook the fettuccine in a large pot of boiling salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • 2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the crushed garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  • 3. Stir in the thickened cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  • 4. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Stir in the chopped parsley and grated parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.
